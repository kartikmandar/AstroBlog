Welcome Post

Kartik Mandar
26th May. 2 min read

It was just a cliche Monday blues thing, and I kept myself busy with the new book I had issued. After all, we only used to get books once per week, so I had to wrap this one before Wednesday. Being so indulged, I didn't notice that there was some new announcement going on in class. My desk partner shook me up, "Kartik, see this. There is a new olympiad known as NAO, and they are sponsoring a trip to NASA for the winners." Damn, that's so cool, I thought while envisioning myself to nail the exam.


"I want in", mine was the first hand to raise. That was the moment that I vividly remember even now, nine years later. It gave origin to a new craft that would, later on, become the guiding light for all my future decisions.


Fast forward all these years, and here I am writing this blog to express and share what I learnt all this time. Still learning and experimenting, making mistakes and improving again.


Now coming on to the point, what will we be exactly exploring in this blog? It could be questions you have unheard of, answers that could shake the very basis of your thinking or just some random tip you need before looking up the sky!

From Galactic Monsters like IC 1101 to the tiny photons, which shows us this beautiful world, we will go on a tour. A tour with no end, it's meaning hiding in the journey. I can be your friend who will try to explore as we go ahead, though you should be the one to take on the leash and redefine it for yourself.
